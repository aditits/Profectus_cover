About Fellowship Program

The fellowship program of Abhyuday gives youth the opportunity to engage themselves in social work by working with NGOs, social startups and alumni of IIT Bombay working in this field. Fellowship focuses on providing an insight into various sectors in the field of social work whilst providing a valuable output for society. The work students do in their fellowship ranges hugely, including communication, impact analysis, fundraising, planning and organizing support groups, help with grant writing and other profiles which are important in socially impactful organisations. Fellows work under the direct supervision of professional social workers or social leaders who have extensive experience in the field.
P.S. Job Profiles like graphic design, digital marketing and web development also open.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the internship?

The duration of the internship is for 1 month from 1st July to 31st July.

What are the steps for register for the fellowship program?

There are 2 ways to register:
1. http://placements.iitb.ac.in/internship/login.jsp.
2. Register directly through the Profectus portal http://abhyudayiitb.org/Profectus.

Are the internships completely work from home?

Yes, the internships are completely work from home.

Is there any limit on the number of internships that one can apply for?

No, one can apply for as many internships as he wishes depending on his interest but fill the preference form which we will send over mail.

Is there any penalty for not completing the internship and leaving it midway?

Yes, leaving an internship midway will get reflected on your resume.

What are the perks offered other than stipend?

Different internships may have different perks like certificate of completion, letter of recommendation, merchandise, flexible working hours etc.

If an intern leaves the internship in between, would he/she receive any stipend or certificate?

No, in that case a stipend or certificate won’t be provided.
